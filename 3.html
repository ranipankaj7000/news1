TMC MP’s SUV mows down 5-year-old boy in Murshidabad

Trinamool Congress member of Parliament (MP), Abu Taher Khan, who represents Murshidabad Lok Sabha constituency, said the child got hit by his vehicle when he was trying to run across the road

A five-year-old boy was killed after allegedly hit by a Sports Utility Vehicle (SUV) in which Trinamool Congress’s member of Parliament (MP), Abu Taher Khan, was travelling in West Bengal’s Murshidabad district on Wednesday afternoon, police said.

Police have arrested the car’s driver, Alamgir Mondol, and the vehicle was also seized. “Further legal procedures are being carried out,” said KS Raj Kumar, superintendent of police (SP) of Murshidabad.
