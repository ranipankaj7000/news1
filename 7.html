Sooraj Barjatya says people walked out of Hum Aapke Hain Koun premiere

Sooraj Barjatya spoke about how people did not like Hum Aapke Hain Koun when it first released in 1994 and many even walked out of its premiere.

By HT Entertainment Desk Sooraj Barjatya’s Hum Aapke Hain Koun broke all records at the box office after its release in 1994. With worldwide earnings of ₹250 crore, it was the highest grossing Indian film ever back then, a tag it held for 15 years. But the film did not have a very rosy start. In fact, it was at the risk of being a commercial failure in its opening weekend. In a new interview, the filmmaker opened up about how he saw people walking out of the film.
